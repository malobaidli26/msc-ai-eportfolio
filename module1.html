Module 1 – Launch into Computing

This page presents the learning activities, reflections, and artefacts developed during Module 1 of the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme at the University of Essex Online.

Module Overview

Module 1 introduced key concepts in computing, including the foundations of software development, algorithms, computational thinking, and the role of computing in modern organisations. The module also explored how computing technologies influence business, society, and digital transformation.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand fundamental computing concepts and principles.
  • Explore the evolution and impact of computing in organisations and society.
  • Develop academic and critical thinking skills.
  • Reflect on learning activities and collaborative discussions.

Artefacts and Activities

  • Collaborative discussion posts.
  • Learning activities and exercises.
  • Algorithm and computing concept analysis.
  • Reflective learning notes.

Reflection

Throughout this module, I developed a deeper understanding of computing principles and their role in real-world applications. The activities and discussions helped strengthen my analytical thinking and allowed me to connect theoretical knowledge with practical scenarios in computing and technology.
