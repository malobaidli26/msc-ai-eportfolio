Reflection

Reflection is an important part of postgraduate learning. Throughout the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme, I continuously reflect on my learning experiences, academic progress, and engagement with module activities.

During Module 1, I developed a stronger understanding of the foundations of computing, including computational thinking, algorithms, and the broader impact of computing technologies on organisations and society.

The collaborative discussions and learning activities helped me connect theoretical concepts with practical scenarios. This reflective process allowed me to evaluate my strengths, identify areas for improvement, and enhance my analytical and critical thinking skills.

Through continuous reflection, I aim to improve my academic performance, deepen my understanding of computing concepts, and further develop my professional and technical capabilities throughout the programme.
